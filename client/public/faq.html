Frequently Asked Questions

What is MereMiners?

MereMiners is a mobile crypto mining game where you earn MERE coins by deploying miners and unlocking slots.

How do I earn MERE?

Place miners in your mining room to generate MERE daily. You can upgrade miners and unlock more slots for higher earnings.

Is MereMiners free to play?

You get a free trial miner when you start. Additional miners and slots can be purchased with MERE coins.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

You can withdraw MERE to USDT using the wallet feature. Follow the instructions in the Wallet section.

Who can I contact for support?

Email our support team at support@mereminers.com for help with your account or technical issues.
